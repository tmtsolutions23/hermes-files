⚡ Master Difficulty Key Differences:
• Tempo windows are ~40% tighter than Amateur
• Slight fast/slow tempo = 5-15 yard error
• Wind gusts vary ±10-30% from displayed
• Lie penalties are MORE severe (rough costs more)
• No putt preview line — must read green manually
• No spin indicator after contact — must feel it
• Fast greens at Very Firm can 3-putt easily
🎯 Master Wind Tips:
• Headwind hurts MORE than tailwind helps (asymmetric)
• Downhill + headwind = wind effect amplified
• Uphill + tailwind = wind effect reduced
• Crosswind drift increases with altitude
• Gust variance: trust 70% of displayed mph for safe play
📊 Elevation Rules of Thumb:
• Short irons/wedges (< 100y): 1y per 4ft elevation
• Mid irons (100-170y): 1y per 3ft elevation
• Long irons/woods (> 170y): 1y per 2.5ft elevation
• Downhill = subtract carry and usually increase rollout
• Ball goes proportionally further downhill
📐 Calculation Formulas:

Wind Scaling (non-linear):
distScale uses stepped curve fitting community data:
<60y: 0.35x | 60-80y: 0.50x | 80-100y: 0.65x | 100-130y: 0.85x
130-170y: 1.00x | 170-200y: 1.10x | 200-230y: 1.25x | 230y+: 1.40x

Headwind: mph × cos(θ) × 1.35 × distScale × shotMult
Tailwind: mph × cos(θ) × 0.75 × distScale × shotMult
Crosswind: mph × sin(θ) × distScale × shotMult × sideMult × spinMult × clubLatMult

Lie Penalty: required carry = (yardage + wind + elevation) ÷ (1 - liePenalty)
Shot Type wind mult: Full 1.00 | Punch 0.65 | Pitch 0.85 | Flop 1.30 | Chip 0.40
Elevation: club-dependent (1y/4ft, 1y/3ft, 1y/2.5ft)
Rollout: base × firmness × spin × shot × lie × downhill/uphill modifier
